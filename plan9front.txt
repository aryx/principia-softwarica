Most of the code in Principia Softwarica comes from the 4th edition
of Plan 9 available at https://9p.io/sources/plan9/sys/src/ and
https://github.com/plan9foundation/plan9

Some code comes also from 9front https://github.com/9front/9front such as:
 - mothra and libpanel (which was present in Plan 9 2nd edition), as well
   as resize, tcs, uhtml
 - improvements to libsec/ and devtls.c to support https://
 - git9, patch (smaller than GNU patch ported via APE), and diff (for diff -u,
   also smaller than the one using APE)
 - paint.c
 - walk.c
 - hold (poor's man editor under rio used in git9)
 - improvements to awk to not depend on APE
 - improvements to getflags.c and usage.c for named command-line arguments
 - improvements to rc to support `$nl{} custom split IFS
 - il.c network protocol (which was present in older Plan 9 edition)
